Our Story!

What is Minot First Robotics?

We are a organization dedicated to inspiring young minds through competitive robotics. Based in Minot, North Dakota, we manage teams across three FIRST leagues.

Our mission is to foster STEM education, teamwork, and innovation. Students learn engineering principles, programming, and business skills while building robots that compete at regional and national levels.
